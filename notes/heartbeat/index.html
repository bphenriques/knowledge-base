    Heartbeat

    In order to detect failures, systems communicate with one another to verify communication. If the communication is deemed broken, then the system may be considered as Unreachable depending on he heartbeat history and how the Failure Detection is configured. I.e., a single heartbeat does not mean that the member is Unreachable.
